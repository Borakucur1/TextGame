Text Me When You're Home

Someone sees you trotting along, not a typical spot to be walking.

“I can drive you,” the stranger says. “It's on my way.”

You start to follow… and your phone buzzes like it's panicking.

“Where are you going?”

Your friend sends a screenshot of your shared location.

“That's not the station.”

Your stomach drops.

You stop walking.

The stranger keeps walking for two steps before noticing you're not there anymore.

You hear, “Hey! Where'd you go?”
