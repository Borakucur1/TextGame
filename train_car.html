Text Me When You're Home

The train finally arrives with a tired metallic scream.

The doors open, and everyone on the platform hesitates for half a second like the train might be a trick.

You step inside.

The car is mostly empty. A man sleeps with his hood pulled over his face. Someone at the far end scrolls their phone with the brightness turned all the way up.

The fluorescent lights flicker once.

Your friend’s voice echoes in your head: Stay where people can see you.

You check your battery.

7%.

The doors chime. You need to decide where to sit.

  • Sit near the conductor and ride it out
  • Move to the empty car next door
  • Get off at the next stop and rethink the route
