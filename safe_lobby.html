Text Me When You're Home

The cashier points you toward a building nearby with a lobby that stays open late.

You step inside and immediately feel the difference.

Warm air. Security cameras. A front desk lamp glowing like a small moon.

The person at the desk looks up, takes one look at you, and says, “You can sit for a minute.”

You do.

Your phone is almost dead, but for the first time tonight, you are not outside deciding things alone.

The elevator hums somewhere behind a closed door.

Your friend texts again:

“Stay there. Do not wander.”

You believe them.

  • Call your friend from the lobby
  • Order a ride from inside
  • Wait until you feel ready, then continue
